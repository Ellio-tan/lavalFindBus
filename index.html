Will you make your bus?

Our application allows you to choose where you are currently located, and we'll do the work of figuring out how many minutes you have before the next bus arrives at the closest bus stop to you.

Currently, our app is useful for those located in Laval, Qu√©bec. Using Laval STL Bus XML feeds, and Fusion Tables storing STL bus stop codes, names, arrival and departure times, we created JavaScript functions to calculate distances and the time you have to get to the nearest bus stop, based on actual (live feed) bus arrival times. Now, you can get where you're going without worrying about missing the next bus. Have a good trip!
